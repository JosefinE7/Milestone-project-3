My Promise To You

There is really something special about the fashion style of the 1940’s-50’s: the beautiful celebration of femininity is really captured during this era with its Squared shoulders, narrow hips, and skirts that ended just below the knee. At Erica Johnsson we want to bring back all the wonderful and fun fashion aspects of this era without making you feel like you are playing dress up, while remaining ethically and environmentally conscious. I am dedicated to making every woman feel beautiful in my clothes, regardless of size or height. You deserve something that was tailored just for you!
